Random Access Memory (RAM)

RAM is a crucial component of every computer system. It temporarily stores data and allows your system to access it quickly, improving the overall performance and multitasking abilities of your device.

Whether you're a gamer, a professional content creator, or just a regular user, having enough RAM is essential to ensure your computer runs smoothly.
